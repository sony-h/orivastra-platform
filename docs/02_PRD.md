# 1. Product Overview

Orivastra is the official digital presence of the Orivastra technology company.

The first version focuses on building a premium company profile website that introduces the company, showcases its expertise, and establishes a strong professional identity.

Future versions will gradually transform the website into a complete digital platform that includes portfolio management, blogs, client portals, AI services, infrastructure dashboards, and intelligent automation.

---

# 2. Objectives

## Business Objectives

- Establish a premium online presence.
- Build trust and credibility.
- Showcase company capabilities.
- Generate leads.
- Prepare the foundation for future products.

---

## Technical Objectives

- Build using modern web technologies.
- Achieve excellent SEO performance.
- Ensure high accessibility.

- Support future expansion without major redesign.

# 12. Assumptions

- Users primarily access from desktop and mobile.
- Future services will share the same platform.
- The website will evolve rather than be rebuilt.
- The project follows a monorepo architecture.

# 15. Product Vision

Orivastra is not intended to remain a static company profile website.

It is designed as the foundation of a complete engineering ecosystem that will eventually integrate software development, cloud infrastructure, artificial intelligence, DevOps, and intelligent automation into a single unified platform.
